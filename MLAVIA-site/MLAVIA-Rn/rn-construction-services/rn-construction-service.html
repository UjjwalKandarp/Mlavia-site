  • BIM

    Merks has been actively using building information modelling (BIM) since the beginning of 2018. BIM is used at all stages of the construction process, from design and planning to production and construction.

    A project developed in the BIM environment provides a more aesthetic and sustainable end product. The added value is a building where nothing is overlooked, because all solutions are aligned and well thought out. In the hands of a responsible team, BIM is a resource that helps to deliver a quality end result to the client faster.

    BIM services

    BIM consultation: We share our knowledge and experience for the successful management of the BIM process.

    BIM modelling process management: BIM models ensure that the design solution is well-elaborated and of high quality.

    BIM in the construction process and administration: We use BIM to control the construction work and develop more efficient cooperation between the parties to the construction process.
